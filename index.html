Welcome to Our Bad Ass Game!

The object is to flip as many tiles to your color as possible!

Player 1 is White. Player 2 is Black.

Flip tiles by bracketing your opponents tiles with your own!

Moves are only possible if they result in a flip.

The player with the highest amount of tiles on the board when there are are no more moves, or the board is full, wins!
